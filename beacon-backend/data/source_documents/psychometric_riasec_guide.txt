================================================================================
MANZIL PLATFORM REFERENCE: PSYCHOMETRIC RIASEC ASSESSMENT GUIDE
================================================================================

This document details the RIASEC personality framework used to match students to suitable career streams.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. OVERVIEW OF THE RIASEC FRAMEWORK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The RIASEC model (developed by psychologist John L. Holland) classifies careers and people into six distinct personality types:
- Realistic (R)
- Investigative (I)
- Artistic (A)
- Social (S)
- Enterprising (E)
- Conventional (C)

A student's RIASEC score profile is derived from the psychometric test. The highest scores (primary and secondary codes) are used to compute career compatibility.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. DETAILED RIASEC CODES & CAREER ALIGNMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. REALISTIC (R) — "THE DOERS"
- Personality Traits: Hands-on, practical, mechanical, outdoors-oriented. They prefer working with tools, microchips, machines, animals, or plants.
- Key Workstyle: "Building" and creating physical/hardware items.
- High-Alignment Careers:
  * Aerospace Engineer, VLSI Semiconductor Engineer, Robotics Engineer.
  * Mechanical, Electrical, Civil Engineers.
  * Veterinary Doctor / Animal Surgeon, Wildlife Biologist.
  * Blockchain Developer, Cybersecurity Analyst (network hardware focus).

B. INVESTIGATIVE (I) — "THE THINKERS"
- Personality Traits: Analytical, curious, research-driven, intellectual. They prefer solving complex mathematical equations, analyzing data, and exploring scientific theories.
- Key Workstyle: "Researching" and analyzing statistics.
- High-Alignment Careers:
  * Data Scientist, AI/ML Engineer, Quantum Computing Scientist.
  * Biomedical Engineer, Bioinformatics Specialist, Astrobiologist.
  * Doctor (MBBS), Research Scientist (Physics/Chemistry), Marine Biologist.
  * Public Policy Analyst, Forensic Psychologist, Actuarial Analyst.

C. ARTISTIC (A) — "THE CREATORS"
- Personality Traits: Expressive, creative, intuitive, non-conforming. They prefer designing layouts, writing storylines, creating animations, and visual communications.
- Key Workstyle: "Creative" and designing.
- High-Alignment Careers:
  * UI/UX Designer, Game Designer, Fashion Designer.
  * Animator / 3D Artist, Film Director / Content Creator.
  * UX Researcher, Digital Marketing Strategist (ad design focus).
  * Architect, Graphic Designer, Brand Manager.

D. SOCIAL (S) — "THE HELPERS"
- Personality Traits: Empathetic, supportive, teaching-oriented, communicative. They prefer instructing students, resolving psychological distress, or leading charity campaigns.
- Key Workstyle: "Helping" and educating.
- High-Alignment Careers:
  * Teacher / Educator, Social Worker / NGO Manager.
  * Clinical / Counselling Psychologist, Genetic Counsellor.
  * Physiotherapist, Doctor (MBBS - patient care focus).
  * HR Manager, International Relations Specialist.

E. ENTERPRISING (E) — "THE PERSUADERS"
- Personality Traits: Assertive, ambitious, leadership-oriented, risk-tolerant. They prefer pitching start-up ideas, managing operations, selling products, and making financial decisions.
- Key Workstyle: "Leading" and presenting.
- High-Alignment Careers:
  * Entrepreneur, Product Manager, Brand Manager.
  * Investment Banker, Stock Broker / Portfolio Manager.
  * Management Consultant, Marketing Manager.
  * IAS / Civil Services Officer, Corporate Lawyer, Sports Manager.

F. CONVENTIONAL (C) — "THE ORGANIZERS"
- Personality Traits: Methodical, structured, detail-oriented, organized. They prefer auditing bank records, organizing logistics, managing data tables, and ensuring legal compliance.
- Key Workstyle: "Structured" and accounting.
- High-Alignment Careers:
  * Chartered Accountant (CA), Financial Risk Manager (FRM), Actuarial Analyst.
  * Operations Manager, Supply Chain Manager.
  * Forensic Accountant, Quality Assurance (QA) Analyst.
  * Law (Legal Advisor/Corporate Compliance), Bank Manager.
